# Terms of Service Gap Analysis (Phase 8)

Date: 2026-03-25
Reviewed File: `src/app/terms/page.tsx`

## Required Accuracy Check

### 1. Client Owns Their Data

Gap: Current terms mention client-owned deliverables in service agreements but do not clearly state that clients own their data in Fleet-Compliance.

### 2. Service Availability Commitments

Gap: Current terms do not describe service availability targets or incident response expectations.

### 3. Data on Cancellation

Gap: Current terms do not state what happens to customer data after cancellation (30-day soft delete, 60-day hard delete, Clerk offboarding).

## Risk Rating

Medium to High. Terms are serviceable for consulting context but incomplete for the SaaS compliance product lifecycle.

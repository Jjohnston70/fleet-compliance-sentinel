# Chief Financial Officer

Use this skill for strategic finance direction, capital reallocation framing, and governance-aware financial planning.

## Use Cases
- Review budget variance and evaluate capital reallocation options.
- Assess financial posture and strategic paths under risk/return tradeoffs.
- Define financial governance checkpoints for executive decisions.

## Output Format

Full:
- Professional Advice Boundary
- Financial Posture Assessment
- Capital Allocation Strategy
- Governance Checkpoints

Quick pass:
- Professional Advice Boundary
- Financial Posture Assessment
- Capital Allocation Strategy
- Governance Checkpoints

Single:
- Professional Advice Boundary
- Financial Posture Assessment
- Capital Allocation Strategy
- Governance Checkpoints

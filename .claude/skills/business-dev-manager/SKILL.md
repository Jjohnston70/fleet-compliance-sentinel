# Business Development Manager

Use this skill to identify and prioritize growth opportunities with partnership and pipeline context.

## Use Cases
- Identify and qualify partnership opportunities in a target market.
- Assess pipeline health and conversion bottlenecks.
- Map market entry strategy with milestones and revenue implications.

## Output Format

Full:
- Growth Objective
- Market and Competitive Assessment
- Opportunity and Partnership Map
- Qualification Criteria
- Pipeline Implications
- Milestones and Cross-Functional Alignment
- Strategic Recommendations
- Next Decision

Quick pass:
- Growth Objective
- Top Opportunities
- Qualification Filters
- Immediate Partnership Moves
- Next Decision

Single:
- Growth Objective
- Requested Output
- Next Decision

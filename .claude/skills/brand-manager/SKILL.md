# Brand Manager

Use this skill to diagnose and correct brand voice and messaging consistency issues across touchpoints.

## Use Cases
- Audit customer-facing touchpoints for voice and messaging consistency.
- Identify gaps between brand standards and actual expression.
- Recommend governance actions to improve brand coherence.

## Output Format

Full:
- Brand Objective
- Touchpoint Inventory
- Voice and Tone Assessment
- Messaging Alignment Gaps
- Priority Corrections
- Governance Recommendations
- Next Decision

Quick pass:
- Brand Objective
- Top Consistency Gaps
- Highest-Impact Corrections
- Next Decision

Single:
- Brand Objective
- Requested Output
- Next Decision

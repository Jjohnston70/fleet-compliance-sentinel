# Sales Strategist

Use this skill to produce strategy-level sales architecture and measurable revenue improvement plans.

## Use Cases
- Design sales motions for defined customer segments.
- Set funnel stage targets and conversion goals.
- Identify high-leverage opportunities to improve pipeline performance.

## Output Format

Full:
- Revenue Objective
- Segment Assessment
- Sales Motion Map
- Funnel Stages and Conversion Targets
- Leverage Opportunities
- Target Metrics and Incentive Alignment
- Strategic Recommendations
- Next Decision

Quick pass:
- Revenue Objective
- Priority Segment
- Primary Sales Motion
- Top Funnel Improvements
- Next Decision

Single:
- Revenue Objective
- Requested Output
- Next Decision

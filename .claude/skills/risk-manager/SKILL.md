# Risk Manager

Use this skill to assess organizational risk exposure and produce mitigation-focused strategy guidance.

## Use Cases
- Identify the highest-risk issues in proposal, delivery, and operations workflows.
- Assess risk likelihood and impact across operational, financial, cyber, and compliance domains.
- Prioritize mitigations aligned to risk appetite and monitoring thresholds.

## Output Format

Full:
- Risk Objective
- Risk Identification
- Probability and Impact
- Mitigation Plan
- Monitoring Signals

Quick pass:
- Risk Objective
- Risk Identification
- Probability and Impact
- Mitigation Plan

Single:
- Risk Objective
- Requested Risk View
- Mitigation Plan

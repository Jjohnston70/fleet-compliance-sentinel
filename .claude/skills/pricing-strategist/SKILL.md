# Pricing Strategist

Use this skill for quantified pricing strategy recommendations that balance growth and margin.

## Use Cases
- Benchmark current pricing against competitors and perceived value.
- Model revenue and margin impact of pricing changes.
- Evaluate tiering, bundling, and packaging options.

## Output Format

Full:
- Pricing Objective
- Current Pricing vs Value Assessment
- Competitive Benchmark
- Cost and Margin Model
- Elasticity and Sensitivity
- Pricing Model Options
- Revenue and Margin Impact
- Recommendation
- Next Decision

Quick pass:
- Pricing Objective
- Competitive Snapshot
- Recommended Price Move
- Expected Revenue Impact
- Next Decision

Single:
- Pricing Objective
- Requested Output
- Next Decision

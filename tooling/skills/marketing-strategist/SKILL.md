# Marketing Strategist

Use this skill to create measurable marketing strategy tied to pipeline and brand outcomes.

## Use Cases
- Plan go-to-market strategy for a new offer.
- Map positioning and channel choices to target segments.
- Design campaign sequencing and budget allocation with ROI signals.

## Output Format

Full:
- Growth Objective
- Target Segments and Buying Triggers
- Competitive Positioning and White Space
- Channel Strategy Map
- Campaign Sequence and Budget Allocation
- Messaging Hierarchy
- Measurement Plan and ROI Signals
- Next Decision

Quick pass:
- Growth Objective
- Priority Segment
- Positioning Statement
- Primary Channel Plan
- Next Decision

Single:
- Growth Objective
- Requested Output
- Next Decision

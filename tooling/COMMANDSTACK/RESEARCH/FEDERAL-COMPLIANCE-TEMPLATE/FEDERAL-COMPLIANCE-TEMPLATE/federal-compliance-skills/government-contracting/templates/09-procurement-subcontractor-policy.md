PROCUREMENT AND SUBCONTRACTOR POLICY

Purpose

This Procurement and Subcontractor Policy establishes [COMPANY_NAME]'s procedures for selecting, managing, and monitoring subcontractors and vendors when performing federal government contracts. This policy ensures compliance with Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR) requirements and maintains the quality and security standards required for federal work.

Scope

This policy applies to all procurement activities and subcontractor relationships established in support of federal contracts. It covers subcontractor selection, flow-down requirements, monitoring procedures, compliance verification, and subcontractor management throughout the contract lifecycle.

Governance and Cross-Package References

This policy establishes federal-specific procurement requirements that overlay general vendor management procedures.

Cross-Package Integration:
security-governance skill: Master governance document establishing vendor management requirements
internal-compliance skill (Vendor Management Policy): General vendor management procedures; this policy adds federal-specific requirements
government-contracting skill (Federal Data Handling and FOIA Policy): Federal data protection requirements for subcontractors

For general vendor management, see internal-compliance skill (Vendor Management Policy).
For federal data protection requirements, see government-contracting skill (Federal Data Handling and FOIA Policy).

Procurement Principles

Fair and Open Competition
Procurement conducted through fair and open competition when practicable
Multiple sources solicited for competitive pricing
Selection based on objective criteria
Documentation of procurement decisions
Avoidance of conflicts of interest

Best Value
Selection based on best value to the government
Consideration of technical capability, past performance, and price
Quality and capability prioritized over lowest price
Total cost of ownership considered
Value analysis documented

Small Business Utilization
Good faith efforts to utilize small businesses as subcontractors
Preference for Service-Disabled Veteran-Owned Small Businesses (SDVOSB)

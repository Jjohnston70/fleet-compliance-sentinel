Purpose

This Statement of Work (SOW) Template provides True North Data Strategies LLC with a standardized format for defining specific project scope, deliverables, timeline, and fees for client engagements. This template ensures clear expectations and project success.

Scope

This template applies to all True North Data Strategies client projects requiring a statement of work. It applies to all personnel involved in project scoping, proposal development, and contract execution.

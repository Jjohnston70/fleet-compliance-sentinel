TRUE NORTH DATA STRATEGIES LLC
TRAINING AND DEVELOPMENT PLAN

Purpose

This Training and Development Plan establishes True North Data Strategies LLC's approach to training and developing personnel. This plan ensures personnel have the knowledge, skills, and competencies needed to perform their roles effectively and support company growth.

Scope

This plan applies to all True North Data Strategies employees, contractors, and consultants. It applies to all personnel involved in training delivery, management, and administration.

Governance and Cross-Package References

This plan integrates security and compliance training requirements into the overall training and development program.

Cross-Package Integration:
Package 2 (Security & Compliance Handbook): Master governance document establishing training requirements
Package 1 (Security Awareness Training Program): Required security training for all personnel
Package 3 (Privacy Management Policy): Privacy training requirements

For security training requirements, see Package 1 (Security Awareness Training Program).

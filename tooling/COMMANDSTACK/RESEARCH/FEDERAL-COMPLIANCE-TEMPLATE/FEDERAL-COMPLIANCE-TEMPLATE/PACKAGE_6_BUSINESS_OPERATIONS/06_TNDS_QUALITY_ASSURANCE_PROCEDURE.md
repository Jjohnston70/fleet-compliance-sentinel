TRUE NORTH DATA STRATEGIES LLC
QUALITY ASSURANCE PROCEDURE

Purpose

This Quality Assurance Procedure establishes True North Data Strategies LLC's approach to ensuring quality in all services, deliverables, and processes. This procedure ensures consistent quality, client satisfaction, and continuous improvement.

Scope

This procedure applies to all True North Data Strategies services, projects, deliverables, and internal processes. It applies to all personnel involved in service delivery, project management, and quality management.

Governance and Cross-Package References

This procedure integrates security and compliance requirements into quality assurance processes.

Cross-Package Integration:
Package 2 (Security & Compliance Handbook): Master governance document; quality assurance includes security and compliance verification
Package 1 (Compliance Management Framework): Compliance requirements integrated into quality assurance
Package 3 (Data Handling Standard): Data quality requirements

For compliance requirements, see Package 2 (Security & Compliance Handbook).

Standards communicated to team

Quality Metrics and Reporting

Quality Metrics

Defect Rate
Definition: Number of defects per deliverable or per 1000 lines of code
Target: [e.g., Less than 2%]
Measurement: Tracked per project and overall

Client Satisfaction
Definition: Client satisfaction rating from surveys or reviews
Target: [e.g., 4.5 out of 5 or higher]
Measurement: Measured per project and quarterly overall

SLA Compliance
Definition: Percentage of SLA targets met
Target: [e.g., 95% or higher]
Measurement: Tracked monthly

Rework Rate
Definition: Percentage of work requiring rework due to quality issues
Target: [e.g., Less than 5%]
Measurement: Tracked per project and overall

On-Time Delivery
Definition: Percentage of deliverables delivered on time
Target: [e.g., 90% or higher]
Measurement: Tracked per project and overall

First-Time Quality
Definition: Percentage of deliverables accepted without rework
Target: [e.g., 90% or higher]
Measurement: Tracked per project and overall

TRUE NORTH DATA STRATEGIES LLC PACKAGE 7 CONTRACTS AND RISK RESEARCH DOCUMENT

Purpose

This research document identifies official legal and standards-based requirements relevant to Package 7 Contracts and Risk documents and validates that True North Data Strategies documentation meets or exceeds these requirements.

Scope

Package 7 contains the following documents:
• Master Services Agreement Template
• Statement of Work Template
• Data Processing Agreement Template
• Business Associate Agreement Template
• Non-Disclosure Agreement Template
• Security Questionnaire Response Template
• Compliance Audit Checklist
• Risk Assessment Template
• Vendor Security Assessment Template
• Incident Response Tabletop Exercise Guide

This research focuses on contractual requirements, privacy and security contract clauses, risk management, and assurance activities.

Official Sources and Requirements

General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) – Processor and Contract Requirements

Source
European Union
Regulation: Regulation (EU) 2016/679

Key Articles
• Article 28: Processor requirements and contracts
• Article 32: Security of processing
• Chapter III: Data subject rights
• Chapter V: International transfers

TNDS Compliance
• Data Processing Agreement Template implements Article 28 requirements, including documented instructions, confidentiality, security, sub-processor conditions, assistance with data subject rights, and audits.
• Master Services Agreement and SOW templates reference and are subordinate to DPA terms for personal data processing.

CCPA/CPRA Service Provider and Contractor Contracts

Source
State of California
California Consumer Privacy Act and Privacy Rights Act

Relevant Requirements
• Contracts must restrict sale or sharing of personal information
• Use of personal information limited to defined business purposes
• Service providers and contractors must provide privacy and security assurances

TNDS Compliance
• Data Processing Agreement Template includes service provider limitations consistent with CCPA/CPRA.
• MSA structure supports inclusion of state-specific addenda where required.

HIPAA Business Associate Agreements

Source
U.S. Department of Health and Human Services (HHS)
Regulations: 45 CFR 164.502(e), 164.504(e), 164.308(b)

Requirements
• Written BAA for PHI handling
• Permitted uses and disclosures of PHI
• Safeguards, reporting, mitigation, access, and amendment support
• Subcontractor BA obligations

TNDS Compliance
• Business Associate Agreement Template includes all required elements for HIPAA-compliant BAAs, including use and disclosure restrictions, safeguard obligations, breach notification, and subcontractor flow-down.

SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria – Risk, Vendor Management, and Assurance

Source
AICPA

Relevant Criteria
• CC3 Risk Assessment
• CC4 Monitoring Activities
• CC6 Vendor and third-party considerations
• CC7 Incident response and testing
• Privacy and confidentiality criteria

TNDS Compliance
• Risk Assessment Template and Vendor Security Assessment Template implement CC3 and CC6 expectations.
• Compliance Audit Checklist and IR Tabletop Exercise Guide implement CC4 and CC7 expectations for monitoring and testing.
• Security Questionnaire Response Template standardizes responses and evidence for external audits and vendor reviews.

NIST SP 800-30 and ISO 31000 Risk Management

Sources
• NIST SP 800-30 Guide for Conducting Risk Assessments
• ISO 31000 Risk Management Guidelines

Requirements
• Identify, analyze, evaluate, and treat risks
• Document risk criteria and acceptance
• Maintain risk register and treatment plans

TNDS Compliance
• Risk Assessment Template aligns with NIST SP 800-30 structure and ISO 31000 principles by documenting likelihood, impact, inherent and residual risk, treatment options, and approvals.
• Outputs feed into audit, corrective actions, and management decisions.

GOOGLE PARTNER COMPLIANCE STATEMENT

Purpose

This Google Partner Compliance Statement documents True North Data Strategies LLC's compliance with Google Partner requirements, security standards, and best practices. This statement demonstrates True North Data Strategies' commitment to maintaining high standards of security, privacy, and service quality as a Google Partner.

Scope

This statement applies to all True North Data Strategies services delivered using Google Workspace and Google Cloud Platform. It applies to all personnel involved in delivering Google-based solutions to clients.

This statement demonstrates TNDS compliance with Google Partner requirements through comprehensive security and compliance programs documented across all packages.

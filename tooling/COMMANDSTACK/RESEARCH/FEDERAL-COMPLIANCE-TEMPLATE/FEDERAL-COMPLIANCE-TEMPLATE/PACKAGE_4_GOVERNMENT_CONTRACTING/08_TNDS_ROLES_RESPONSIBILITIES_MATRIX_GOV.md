TRUE NORTH DATA STRATEGIES LLC
ROLES AND RESPONSIBILITIES MATRIX (GOVERNMENT VERSION)

Purpose

This Roles and Responsibilities Matrix defines the organizational structure, key personnel, responsibilities, authority levels, and backup assignments for True North Data Strategies LLC when performing federal government contracts. This matrix ensures clear accountability and continuity of operations.

Scope

This matrix applies to all True North Data Strategies personnel supporting federal contracts. It covers primary roles, backup personnel, responsibilities, authority levels, decision-making authority, and escalation procedures.

Governance and Cross-Package References

This matrix establishes federal-specific roles and responsibilities that implement the role combination principles documented in Package 2 (Security & Compliance Handbook).

Role Combination Note:
Package 2 (Security & Compliance Handbook) documents TNDS's role combination approach for small business operations
CISO, CIO, CTO, Privacy Officer, and Compliance Officer roles may be combined with documented conflict mitigation
This matrix shows how roles are assigned for federal contract performance

Cross-Package Integration:
Package 2 (Security & Compliance Handbook): Master governance document establishing role requirements and combination principles
Package 1 (Internal Compliance): Operational roles implementing security and compliance controls

Authority Levels and Decision-Making

Level 1 — Executive Authority
Held By: Chief Executive Officer
Scope: All company decisions, contract commitments, financial commitments, personnel decisions
Approval Required From: Board of Directors (if applicable) for major decisions
Delegation: May delegate specific authorities to other roles

Level 2 — Program Authority
Held By: Program Manager, CISO, Compliance Officer
Scope: Program-level decisions, resource allocation within budget, technical and security decisions
Approval Required From: Chief Executive Officer for decisions outside delegated authority
Delegation: May delegate specific authorities to project-level roles

Level 3 — Project Authority
Held By: Project Manager, Technical Leads
Scope: Project-level decisions, task assignments, day-to-day operations
Approval Required From: Program Manager for decisions outside delegated authority
Delegation: Limited delegation to team members for specific tasks

Level 4 — Operational Authority
Held By: Individual contributors, specialists
Scope: Assigned tasks and responsibilities, routine operations
Approval Required From: Project Manager or Technical Lead for decisions outside assigned tasks
Delegation: No delegation authority

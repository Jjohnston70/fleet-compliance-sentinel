# BUSINESS OPERATIONS USAGE GUIDE

## Purpose

This usage guide explains how, when, and why each document in the Business Operations skill is used. This guide helps personnel understand the practical application of business operations documents.

## Skill Overview

The Business Operations skill contains 10 comprehensive documents that establish business processes and operational procedures. These documents are essential for:
- Client onboarding and offboarding
- Employee onboarding and offboarding
- Service delivery and project management
- Pricing and service offerings
- Business development and sales
- Operational efficiency and consistency
- Integration of security and compliance into business processes

This skill serves as the "business operations layer" that integrates security, privacy, and compliance requirements into day-to-day business activities.

## Document Usage Guide

### 01 EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING PROCEDURE

**How Used**
- Guides new employee onboarding process from offer to first day
- Establishes pre-arrival, first day, first week, and first month activities
- Documents security training and access provisioning
- Provides onboarding checklist and timeline
- Ensures consistent employee onboarding experience

**When Needed**
- When hiring new employees or contractors
- During employee onboarding activities
- When establishing employee access and accounts
- During security and compliance training
- When provisioning cloud workspace and cloud platform access
- During onboarding process reviews and improvements

**Why Needed**
- Required for consistent employee onboarding
- Ensures security training and access control compliance
- Implements skill 2 security awareness requirements
- Supports skill 1 access control procedures
- Required for SOC 2 and ISO 27001 compliance (personnel security)
- Establishes accountability for employee security
- Provides foundation for productive employee start

## Cross-Skill Integration

### Skill 2 (Security & Compliance Handbook):
- Skill 2 provides security and compliance requirements; skill 6 integrates them into business processes
- All skill 6 documents reference skill 2 for security requirements
- Skill 2 establishes controls; skill 6 operationalizes them in business delivery

### Skill 1 (Internal Compliance):
- Skill 1 provides operational security procedures; skill 6 integrates them into business processes
- Employee onboarding/offboarding (skill 6) implements access control procedures (skill 1)
- Project management (skill 6) integrates security requirements (skill 1)

### Skill 3 (Data Handling & Privacy):
- Skill 3 provides data handling and privacy requirements; skill 6 integrates them into business processes
- Client onboarding (skill 6) collects data classification requirements (skill 3)
- Client offboarding (skill 6) implements data lifecycle management (skill 3)
- All business processes follow skill 3 retention and deletion rules

### Skill 4 (Government Contracting):
- Skill 4 provides federal overlay; skill 6 provides general business processes
- Federal contract closeout (skill 4) is federal version of client offboarding (skill 6)
- Federal roles matrix (skill 4) is federal version of general roles (skill 6)

### Skill 5 (Cloud Platform Security):
- Skill 5 provides platform security; skill 6 uses platforms for business delivery
- Employee onboarding (skill 6) includes cloud workspace provisioning (skill 5)
- Client onboarding (skill 6) may include cloud platform provisioning (skill 5)

### Skill 7 (Advanced Compliance):
- Skill 7 provides compliance templates; skill 6 provides business processes
- Service Level Agreements (skill 7) reference service offerings (skill 6)
- Master Services Agreement (skill 7) governs client relationships (skill 6)

## Practical Usage Scenarios

### Scenario 1: New Employee Hire
**New employee hired, need to onboard securely and efficiently**
- Response: Use Employee Onboarding Procedure
- Integrate with: skill 1 access control, skill 2 security training, skill 5 cloud provisioning
- Result: Secure and productive employee start

### Scenario 2: Employee Departure
**Employee resigns, need to offboard securely**
- Response: Use Employee Offboarding Procedure
- Integrate with: skill 1 access revocation, skill 3 data protection, skill 5 cloud deprovisioning
- Result: Secure employee departure with business continuity

### Scenario 3: New Client Engagement
**New client signs contract, need to onboard properly**
- Response: Use Client Onboarding Procedure
- Integrate with: skill 3 data classification, skill 2 security assessment, skill 5 platform provisioning
- Result: Secure client onboarding with clear expectations

### Scenario 4: Client Engagement Completion
**Client engagement ending, need to offboard properly**
- Response: Use Client Offboarding Procedure
- Integrate with: skill 3 data lifecycle management, skill 3 records retention, skill 5 access revocation
- Result: Secure client departure with proper data handling

### Scenario 5: Proposal Development
**Prospect requests proposal for services**
- Response: Use Proposal Template and Guide, Service Offerings, Pricing Guide
- Integrate with: skill 2 security capabilities, skill 4 federal capabilities (if applicable)
- Result: Professional proposal with clear services and pricing

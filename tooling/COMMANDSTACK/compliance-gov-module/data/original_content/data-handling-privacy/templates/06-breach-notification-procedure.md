BREACH NOTIFICATION PROCEDURE

Purpose

This Breach Notification Procedure establishes [COMPANY_LEGAL_NAME]'s requirements and procedures for identifying, assessing, responding to, and notifying affected parties of data breaches involving personal information. This procedure ensures timely and appropriate notification in compliance with applicable laws and contractual obligations.

Scope

This procedure applies to all [COMPANY_NAME] personnel, contractors, subcontractors, systems, and processes. It covers breaches of personal information including Personally Identifiable Information (PII), Protected Health Information (PHI), financial information, and other sensitive personal data.

Definitions

Data Breach
Unauthorized or unlawful access, acquisition, use, disclosure, modification, or destruction of personal information, or accidental loss or destruction of personal information. Includes both intentional attacks and accidental incidents.

Personal Information
Information that identifies or can be used to identify an individual including name, Social Security number, driver's license number, financial account information, medical information, email address, or other identifiers.

Breach of Unsecured Personal Information
Breach of personal information that is not rendered unusable, unreadable, or indecipherable through encryption or other methods meeting regulatory standards.

Harm Threshold
The level of risk of harm to individuals that triggers notification requirements. Varies by jurisdiction and regulation.

Notification Trigger
The event or determination that requires notification to affected individuals, regulators, or other parties.

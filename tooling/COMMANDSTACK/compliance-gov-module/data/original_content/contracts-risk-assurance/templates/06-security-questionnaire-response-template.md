1. Organizational Security

1.1 Does your organization have a Chief Information Security Officer (CISO) or equivalent role?
Yes. [OWNER_NAME] serves as CISO and is responsible for overall information security program.

1.2 Does your organization have a formal information security program?
Yes. [COMPANY_NAME] maintains a comprehensive information security program aligned with NIST Cybersecurity Framework and NIST SP 800-53 Rev 5.

1.3 Does your organization have documented information security policies?
Yes. [COMPANY_NAME] maintains documented policies including Information Security Policy, Acceptable Use Policy, Access Control Policy, Data Handling Policy, Incident Response Plan, Business Continuity Plan, and others.

1.4 How often are security policies reviewed and updated?
Security policies are reviewed annually and updated as needed based on regulatory changes, threat landscape changes, or organizational changes.

1.5 Does your organization provide security awareness training to employees?
Yes. All employees receive security awareness training during onboarding and annually thereafter. Training covers phishing, password security, data protection, incident reporting, and other security topics.

1.6 Does your organization conduct background checks on employees?
Yes. Background checks are conducted on all employees prior to employment and access to sensitive systems or data.

1.7 Do employees sign confidentiality agreements?
Yes. All employees sign confidentiality agreements as part of onboarding.

2. Access Control

2.1 Does your organization implement role-based access control?
Yes. Access to systems and data is granted based on job role and least privilege principle.

2.2 Does your organization require multi-factor authentication (MFA)?
Yes. MFA is required for all user accounts accessing company systems and data.

2.3 How often are user access rights reviewed?
User access rights are reviewed quarterly and when employees change roles or leave the organization.

2.4 Does your organization have a process for provisioning and deprovisioning user access?
Yes. User access is provisioned during onboarding per Access Control Policy and deprovisioned immediately upon termination per Employee Offboarding Procedure.

2.5 Does your organization enforce password complexity requirements?
Yes. Password requirements include: Minimum 12 characters, combination of uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters, no reuse of last 12 passwords, 90-day expiration for privileged accounts.

2.6 Does your organization use privileged access management?
Yes. Privileged access is strictly controlled, monitored, and audited. Privileged accounts require MFA and are subject to enhanced monitoring.

3. Data Protection

3.1 Does your organization classify data based on sensitivity?
Yes. Data is classified as Public, Internal, Confidential, or Restricted per Data Handling Standard.

3.2 Does your organization encrypt data at rest?
Yes. Sensitive data at rest is encrypted using AES-256 encryption.

3.3 Does your organization encrypt data in transit?
Yes. Data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.

3.4 Does your organization have a data retention and disposal policy?
Yes. Data retention and disposal requirements are defined in Records Management Policy. Data is retained per legal and business requirements and securely disposed of when no longer needed.

3.5 Does your organization conduct data loss prevention (DLP)?
Yes. DLP controls are implemented to prevent unauthorized data exfiltration, including email filtering, endpoint controls, and cloud DLP.

3.6 Does your organization have a process for handling personal data?
Yes. Personal data is handled per Data Handling Standard and Privacy Management Policy in compliance with GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable privacy laws.

3.7 Does your organization have a data breach response plan?
Yes. Data breach response procedures are defined in Incident Response Plan and Breach Notification Procedure.

4. Network Security

4.1 Does your organization use firewalls?
Yes. Firewalls are deployed at network perimeter and between network segments.

4.2 Does your organization use intrusion detection/prevention systems (IDS/IPS)?
Yes. IDS/IPS systems monitor network traffic for malicious activity.

4.3 Does your organization segment networks?
Yes. Networks are segmented to isolate sensitive systems and data.

4.4 Does your organization use VPN for remote access?
Yes. VPN is required for remote access to company systems from untrusted networks.

4.5 Does your organization monitor network traffic?
Yes. Network traffic is monitored continuously for security threats and anomalies.

4.6 Does your organization conduct vulnerability scanning?
Yes. Vulnerability scanning is conducted monthly for all systems. Critical vulnerabilities are remediated within 15 days, high within 30 days.

4.7 Does your organization conduct penetration testing?
Yes. Penetration testing is conducted annually by qualified third-party security firm.

5. Endpoint Security

5.1 Does your organization use antivirus/anti-malware software?
Yes. Antivirus/anti-malware software is deployed on all endpoints and updated automatically.

5.2 Does your organization use endpoint detection and response (EDR)?
Yes. EDR solutions monitor endpoints for malicious activity and enable rapid response.

5.3 Does your organization encrypt endpoint devices?
Yes. All laptops and mobile devices are encrypted using full-disk encryption.

5.4 Does your organization have a mobile device management (MDM) policy?
Yes. Mobile devices accessing company data are managed per Mobile Device Policy and BYOD Policy.

5.5 Does your organization patch endpoints regularly?
Yes. Security patches are applied per Patch Management Procedure. Critical patches within 15 days, high within 30 days, medium within 60 days.

6. Application Security

6.1 Does your organization follow secure development practices?
Yes. Secure coding practices are followed for all software development, including input validation, output encoding, authentication, authorization, error handling, and logging.

6.2 Does your organization conduct code reviews?
Yes. Code reviews are conducted for all code changes before deployment.

6.3 Does your organization conduct application security testing?
Yes. Application security testing includes static analysis, dynamic analysis, and penetration testing.

6.4 Does your organization use web application firewalls (WAF)?
Yes. WAF is deployed for web applications to protect against common attacks (SQL injection, XSS, CSRF, etc.).

6.5 Does your organization have a vulnerability disclosure program?
Yes. Security vulnerabilities can be reported to [CONTACT_EMAIL]. Vulnerabilities are triaged, remediated, and disclosed per Vulnerability Management Procedure.

8. Incident Response

8.1 Does your organization have an incident response plan?

8.4 Does your organization conduct incident response exercises?
Yes. Tabletop exercises are conducted annually to test incident response procedures.

8.5 Does your organization have a process for notifying affected parties of security incidents?

9. Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery

9.1 Does your organization have a business continuity plan?
Yes. Business Continuity Plan defines procedures for maintaining operations during disruptions.

9.2 Does your organization have a disaster recovery plan?

9.3 Does your organization conduct backups?

BUSINESS CONTINUITY PLAN

Purpose

This Business Continuity Plan establishes [COMPANY_LEGAL_NAME]'s framework for maintaining critical business operations during and after a disruption. This plan ensures the company can continue to serve clients, protect information assets, and recover normal operations following emergencies, disasters, or other significant disruptions.

Scope

This plan applies to all [COMPANY_NAME] personnel, systems, facilities, and business operations. It covers disruptions including natural disasters, cyberattacks, equipment failures, pandemics, utility outages, and other events that could impact business operations.

Governance and Cross-Package References

Business Continuity Strategies

Remote Work Capability
All personnel equipped for remote work
Cloud-based systems accessible from any location
Virtual Private Network (VPN) for secure remote access
Collaboration tools (Google Workspace) for distributed teams
Remote work policies and procedures documented

Alternate Work Locations
Primary: Home offices of personnel
Secondary: Co-working spaces or temporary office space
Tertiary: Client sites (if appropriate and available)
Alternate locations identified and documented
Alternate location access procedures established

System Redundancy and High Availability
Critical systems hosted in cloud with high availability
Geographic redundancy for critical systems
Load balancing for availability
Failover capabilities for critical systems
Redundancy tested regularly

Data Backup and Recovery
Automated daily backups of critical data
Backups stored in geographically separate cloud location
Backup encryption and access controls
Regular backup restoration testing (quarterly)
Backup retention per data retention policy (30-90 days)

Communication Systems
Multiple communication channels (email, phone, video conferencing, messaging)
Cloud-based communication systems accessible remotely
Emergency notification system for personnel
Client communication procedures
Backup communication methods identified

Vendor and Supplier Relationships
Critical vendors identified
Alternate vendors identified where feasible
Vendor contact information maintained
Vendor business continuity capabilities assessed
Vendor agreements include business continuity requirements

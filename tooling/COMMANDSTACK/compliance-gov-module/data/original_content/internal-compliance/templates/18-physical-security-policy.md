PHYSICAL SECURITY POLICY

Purpose

This Physical Security Policy establishes [COMPANY_LEGAL_NAME]'s requirements for protecting physical assets, facilities, and personnel from unauthorized access, theft, damage, and other physical threats. This policy ensures appropriate physical security controls are implemented to protect company resources.

Scope

This policy applies to all [COMPANY_NAME] facilities, offices, work locations, and physical assets. It applies to all personnel, visitors, contractors, and any other individuals accessing company facilities or handling company assets.

Governance and Cross-Package References

This policy operates under the authority of the Security and Compliance Handbook (security-governance skill), which establishes the master governance framework for physical security.

Universal [COMPANY_ABBREVIATION] Principles:

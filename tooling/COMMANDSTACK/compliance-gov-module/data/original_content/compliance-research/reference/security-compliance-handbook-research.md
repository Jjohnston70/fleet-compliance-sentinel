# SKILL 2: SECURITY AND COMPLIANCE HANDBOOK RESEARCH DOCUMENT

## Purpose

This research document identifies official requirements from authoritative sources for Skill 2 Security and Compliance Handbook and validates that organizational documentation meets or exceeds these requirements.

## Scope

Skill 2 consists of the Security and Compliance Handbook, which defines the governance structure, roles, responsibilities, risk management approach, and overall information security and privacy program. This research document focuses on governance, risk management, oversight, and program-level control requirements.

## Official Sources and Requirements

### NIST Cybersecurity Framework

**Source**
National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)
Website: https://www.nist.gov/cyberframework

**Document:** Framework for Improving Critical Infrastructure Cybersecurity

**Relevant Functions and Categories**
- Identify (all categories)
- Protect (PR.IP Information Protection Processes and Procedures)
- Detect (DE.CM Security Continuous Monitoring)
- Respond (RS.RP Response Planning)
- Recover (RC.RP Recovery Planning)
- ID.GV Governance
- ID.RA Risk Assessment
- ID.RM Risk Management Strategy

**Compliance Assessment**
- The Security and Compliance Handbook establishes the organizational cybersecurity risk management function, governance structure, and risk appetite in alignment with NIST CSF Identify and Governance categories.
- The Handbook defines the requirement to implement, review, and improve the control environment, covering all CSF functions at the program level.

### NIST SP 800-53 Revision 5 Program and Governance Controls

**Source**
National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)
Website: https://csrc.nist.gov/publications/detail/sp/800-53/rev-5/final

**Document:** Security and Privacy Controls for Information Systems and Organizations

**Relevant Control Families**
- Program Management (PM)
- Planning (PL)
- Assessment, Authorization, and Monitoring (CA)
- Risk Assessment (RA)
- Security and Privacy Program Controls in other families (e.g., SR, PS, AT)

**Key Controls Addressed**
- PM-1: Information Security Program Plan
- PM-3: Information Security and Privacy Resources
- PM-8: Critical Infrastructure Plan
- PL-1: Security and Privacy Planning Policy and Procedures
- CA-1: Security and Privacy Assessment Policy and Procedures
- RA-1: Risk Assessment Policy and Procedures
- RA-3: Risk Assessment
- RA-7: Risk Response

**Compliance Assessment**
- The Security and Compliance Handbook functions as the overarching Information Security and Privacy Program Plan required by PM-1.
- The Handbook defines risk assessment, risk treatment, and monitoring practices aligned with RA and CA families.
- Roles, responsibilities, and resource expectations for security and privacy are clearly defined consistent with PM-3.

### ISO/IEC 27001 and ISO/IEC 27002

**Source**
International Organization for Standardization (ISO)
Website: https://www.iso.org

**Standards:** ISO/IEC 27001 Information Security Management Systems; ISO/IEC 27002 Code of Practice for Information Security Controls

**Relevant Clauses and Domains**
- ISO 27001 Clauses 4–10 (Context, Leadership, Planning, Support, Operation, Performance Evaluation, Improvement)
- Annex A Domain A.5 Information Security Policies
- Annex A Domain A.6 Organization of Information Security
- Annex A Domain A.18 Compliance

**Compliance Assessment**
- The Security and Compliance Handbook defines scope, context, leadership commitment, continual improvement, and policy framework aligned with ISO 27001 clause structure.
- The Handbook assigns security responsibilities and governance bodies consistent with Annex A.6.
- The Handbook identifies compliance obligations and oversight consistent with Annex A.18.

### SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria – Governance and Risk

**Source**
American Institute of CPAs (AICPA)
Website: https://www.aicpa.org

**Framework:** SOC 2 Trust Services Criteria

**Relevant Criteria**
- CC1 Control Environment
- CC2 Communication and Information
- CC3 Risk Assessment
- CC4 Monitoring Activities
- CC5 Control Activities at the program level

**Compliance Assessment**
- The Security and Compliance Handbook documents tone at the top, roles, responsibilities, and oversight aligned with CC1.
- The Handbook mandates communication of policies and control expectations, aligning with CC2.
- The risk assessment and monitoring requirements in the Handbook align with CC3 and CC4.

### COSO Internal Control – Integrated Framework

**Source**
Committee of Sponsoring Organizations of the Treadway Commission (COSO)
Website: https://www.coso.org

**Relevant Components**
- Control Environment
- Risk Assessment
- Control Activities
- Information and Communication
- Monitoring Activities

**Compliance Assessment**
- The Security and Compliance Handbook mirrors COSO's five components at the information security and privacy program level.
- Governance structures, committee definitions, and escalation paths support a strong control environment and monitoring.

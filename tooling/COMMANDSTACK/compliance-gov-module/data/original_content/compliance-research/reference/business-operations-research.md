# SKILL 6: BUSINESS AND OPERATIONS RESEARCH DOCUMENT

## Purpose

This research document identifies official and recognized best practice sources relevant to Skill 6 Business and Operations documents and validates that organizational documentation meets or exceeds these requirements.

## Scope

Skill 6 contains the following documents:
- Employee Onboarding Procedure
- Employee Offboarding Procedure
- Project Initiation Template
- Client Onboarding Procedure
- Service Level Agreement Template
- Quality Assurance Procedure
- Document Control Procedure
- Training and Development Plan
- Performance Management Procedure
- Communication and Collaboration Standards

This research focuses on operational governance, quality, documentation, training, and service management.

## Official Sources and Requirements

### ISO 9001 Quality Management Systems

**Source**
International Organization for Standardization (ISO)
**Standard:** ISO 9001 Quality Management Systems

**Relevant Requirements**
- Documented processes and procedures
- Control of documented information
- Competence, training, and awareness
- Design and development planning
- Performance evaluation and continual improvement

**Compliance Assessment**
- Quality Assurance Procedure, Document Control Procedure, and Training and Development Plan implement quality and competence processes consistent with ISO 9001 concepts.
- Performance Management and project/client onboarding processes support continual improvement and consistent delivery.

### ISO/IEC 27001 Supporting Clauses

**Source**
International Organization for Standardization (ISO)
**Standard:** ISO/IEC 27001 Information Security Management Systems

**Relevant Clauses and Annex Controls**
- A.6 Organization of Information Security
- A.7 Human Resource Security (prior to, during, and after employment)
- A.8 Asset Management
- A.12 Operations Security
- A.18 Compliance and documentation management

**Compliance Assessment**
- Employee Onboarding and Offboarding Procedures support human resource security controls aligned with Annex A.7.
- Document Control Procedure and Communication Standards implement information handling and documentation practices aligned with Annex A.12 and A.18.

### Project and Service Management Guidance

**Sources**
- Project Management Institute (PMI) A Guide to the Project Management Body of Knowledge (PMBOK Guide)
- ITIL Service Management Practices

**Relevant Concepts**
- Project initiation, scope, roles, and communication planning
- Service level definition and monitoring
- Continual service improvement

**Compliance Assessment**
- Project Initiation Template and Client Onboarding Procedure define project scope, roles, communication, and risk consistent with PMBOK concepts.
- Service Level Agreement Template defines service levels, availability, incident response, and reporting consistent with service management practices.

### Training and Workforce Development Guidance

**Sources**
- NIST Special Publication 800-50 Building an Information Technology Security Awareness and Training Program
- General best practice for competency management

**Compliance Assessment**
- Training and Development Plan includes security awareness, role-based training, and tracking consistent with NIST SP 800-50.
- Performance Management Procedure integrates training and performance expectations.

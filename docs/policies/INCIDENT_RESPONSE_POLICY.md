## Purpose

This policy defines incident response requirements for Fleet-Compliance services and regulated customer data.

## Source Procedure

Operational procedures are defined in `INCIDENT_RESPONSE_PLAN.md`. This policy defines control requirements and minimum standards.

## Incident Classification

Incidents are classified as:

- P1 Critical
- P2 High
- P3 Medium
- P4 Low

Classification criteria and response expectations are maintained in the incident response plan and must be followed for every incident.

## Required Response Actions

For all incidents:

1. Open an incident record.
2. Classify severity.
3. Assign an incident owner.
4. Capture timeline and response actions.
5. Record resolution and follow-up actions.

## Breach Notification Requirements

For confirmed or suspected data breaches:

- Notify affected clients within 72 hours.
- Assess GDPR Article 33 reporting threshold.
- Notify the relevant supervisory authority within 72 hours when required.

## Documentation and Evidence

All incidents must be documented under `SECURITY_REPORTS/incidents/` with:

- Detection source
- Scope and affected systems
- Containment actions
- Root cause
- Corrective actions
- Owner and due dates

## Post-Incident Review

- P1 and P2 incidents require formal review.
- Review must occur within 7 days.
- Action items are tracked to closure.

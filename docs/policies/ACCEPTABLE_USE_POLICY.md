## Purpose

This policy defines permitted and prohibited use of Fleet-Compliance services.

## Intended Use

Fleet-Compliance is intended for lawful fleet and DOT compliance operations, including:

- Driver qualification and compliance tracking
- Fleet asset and permit tracking
- Invoice and maintenance support records
- Compliance workflow management under FMCSA-related requirements

## Prohibited Use

Users may not:

- Access data outside their authorized organization
- Upload malware or attempt unauthorized system access
- Use the service for illegal activity
- Store data unrelated to approved business use
- Circumvent billing, access, or security controls

## Data Allowed

The platform may store operational compliance data, including:

- Driver records and qualification data
- Compliance dates and permit records
- Fleet asset records
- Invoices and maintenance-related business records

## Data Not Allowed

Users must not intentionally store:

- Personal financial account credentials
- Full payment card data
- Data unrelated to fleet/compliance operations
- Any data they are not legally authorized to process

## User Responsibilities

Users are responsible for:

- Keeping credentials secure
- Using accurate and current data
- Reporting suspected misuse or security events promptly
- Following retention and deletion requirements

## Enforcement

Violations may result in account suspension, access termination, and contractual or legal action.

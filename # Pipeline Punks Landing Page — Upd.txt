multi-vertical. The plumbing is done. Fleet is just what's loaded in it.

active, telematics integrated, real CFR knowledge indexed, billing layer live.
That is the asset. The realty and govcon verticals are close behind — the knowledge
and modules exist, they need a domain-specific dashboard config and knowledge load.
